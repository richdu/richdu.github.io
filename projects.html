Classroom Salon is an education platform built by Princeton and CMU. I created the mobile viewer. Students can post comments and annotations on videos, which are then used to generate analytics. It also provides transcript and video clip navigation. It's powered by AngularJS and ASP.NET.

Princeton Summer Storage

A sizable portion of Princeton students are international or live far away from Princeton (e.g. California). When these students go home for the summer, where does their stuff go? Mostly to companies that offer summer storage at rather high prices. This website matches students with other students who have space over the summer for storage.
